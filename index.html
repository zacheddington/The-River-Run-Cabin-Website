About the Property

Nestled right on the banks of Henry's Fork of the Snake River, The River Run Cabin offers a true angler's paradise and a peaceful retreat for nature lovers. Step out your door to immediate river access—perfect for fishing, floating, or simply soaking in the tranquil views. The property is just a minute from the main river take-out, making it effortless to enjoy a day on the water. After your adventures, store your fishing gear and unwind by the cozy fireplace or fire up the grill for a riverside meal. Whether you're seeking world-class fly fishing, a family getaway, or a relaxing escape surrounded by the beauty of Idaho, The River Run Cabin is your home on the river.
